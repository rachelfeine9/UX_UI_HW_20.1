Welcome to Echo Hill Ranch Gold Star

Echo Hill Ranch is a camp 18 miles southwest of Kerrville, TX. 200+ acres of pristine Texas Hill Country owned by the Friedman family since 1952.


Our mission is to provide a safe, fun, relaxing summer camp experience for the children of Gold Star families.

Activity List


Archery

Campers will learn how to use an old-fashioned bow, while shooting their arrows at large standing targets on our Archery Range.

Riflery

Campers will learn how to safely shoot .177 & .22 rifles on our newly renovated Riflery Range.

Nature Study

Campers will explore the beautiful nature of our little green valley.

Handicrafts

Campers will harness their creativity with activities that include: making friendship bracelets, tie-dying clothes, making sand art, painting with watercolors, making dreamcatchers, and creating chalk art.

Leatherworks

Campers will help bring back a more forgotten art-form, by imprinting metal stamps onto pieces of cut leather, providing them with unique keepsakes that will last for years to come.

Swimming

Campers will cool off everyday, during free-swim, in our natural spring-fed, Wallace Creek.

Horseback Riding

Campers are always excited about our horseback riding activity. Getting to ride a horse while truly out in nature, is an experience campers never forget.

Sports

Campers are always active on the ranch and will enjoy sports such as: fishing, canoeing, waterpolo, basketball, soccor, tennis, kickball, and teatherball.

Frequently Asked Questions


WHERE DO THE CHILDREN LIVE?

Housing arrangements are western-style bunkhouses – each with its own bathroom containing sinks, showers, and commodes. Each cabin has hot and cold water. Counselors live in cabins with the children.

WHERE DO THE CHILDREN SWIM?

The children will enjoy nearly a mile of Big Foot Wallace Creek, a spring-fed tributary of the Guadalupe River. The two main swimming areas are crystal clear waters controlled by rock dams. All waterfront activities are overseen by well-trained staff.

IS THERE HORSEBACK RIDING?

Yes – children ride on horses from a carefully selected string appropriate for new riders. The horseback riding program is managed by professional riding staff to care for the horses properly and ensure the children have the training and equipment to ride safely.

WHERE DO THE CHILDREN EAT?

All meals are prepared on-site by certified and experienced staff. Children eat family style with their bunkhouse groups. Meals are balanced and healthy while including favorites such as fried chicken, spaghetti, and pizza. Dinner is typically served in an outside picnic area with hot dogs, hamburgers, a veggie option, and ice-cold watermelon.

WHO ARE THE STAFF?

Camping professionals with extensive experience are the program leaders. Each special activity is taught by experts. Staff have experience with Echo Hill Ranch, camping, and counseling. The camp has a fully equipped infirmary with a nurse on staff and a doctor on call.
